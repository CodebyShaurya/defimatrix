# 5 new interesting Ethereum tokens you should pay attention to in 2024

The Ethereum ecosystem continues to evolve with innovative token projects that are pushing the boundaries of decentralized finance. Here are five noteworthy tokens that deserve your attention in 2024.

The native token of the DeFiMatrix protocol, DMX combines AI-powered intent execution with traditional DeFi mechanics:

- Cross-chain compatibility
- AI-driven yield optimization
- Governance rights
- Fee sharing mechanism
- Staking rewards

## 2. AutoHedge (AHG)

An innovative token powering automated hedging strategies:

- AI-based risk management

## 3. CrossBridge (XBRD)

Facilitating seamless cross-chain transactions:

- Bridge fee reduction
- Security staking
- Governance participation

- Cross-chain messaging

## 4. DataFi (DTF)

Tokenizing data management in DeFi:

- Privacy-preserving computations

- AI model training incentives

Focusing on sustainable blockchain operations:

- Carbon offset mechanisms
- Renewable energy credits
- Sustainable mining rewards
- Environmental impact tracking
- Green project funding
