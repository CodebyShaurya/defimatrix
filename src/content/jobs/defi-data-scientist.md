# About DeFiMatrix

DeFiMatrix is revolutionizing decentralized finance through AI-powered automation and data-driven decision making. Our platform leverages advanced analytics and machine learning to optimize DeFi strategies and provide users with intelligent, automated financial solutions.

## Role Overview

As a DeFi Data Scientist, you'll be responsible for building and implementing sophisticated data models that power our platform's decision-making capabilities. You'll work with large-scale blockchain data to derive insights, develop predictive models, and optimize DeFi strategies across multiple protocols and chains.

## Key Responsibilities

- Design and implement machine learning models for DeFi strategy optimization
- Analyze on-chain data to identify patterns, opportunities, and risks
- Develop predictive models for market behavior and protocol performance
- Create and maintain data pipelines for real-time blockchain data processing
- Build risk assessment models for DeFi protocols and strategies
- Collaborate with the AI team to enhance our intent recognition systems
- Develop metrics and KPIs for measuring strategy performance
- Create visualizations and dashboards for internal and external stakeholders

## Required Skills & Experience

- 3+ years of experience in data science or related field
- Strong background in machine learning and statistical analysis
- Proficiency in Python and data science libraries (pandas, scikit-learn, etc.)
- Experience with big data technologies and SQL
- Understanding of DeFi protocols and blockchain technology
- Strong mathematical and analytical skills
- Experience with data visualization tools
- Excellent problem-solving abilities

## Nice to Have

- Experience with blockchain data analysis tools (Dune, The Graph, etc.)
- Knowledge of financial markets and trading strategies
- Familiarity with deep learning frameworks
- Experience with real-time data processing
- Background in quantitative finance
- Contributions to data science research or publications

## What We Offer

- Comprehensive health and wellness benefits

- Access to cutting-edge data science tools and technologies
- Collaborative and innovative team culture
