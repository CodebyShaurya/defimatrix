# About DeFiMatrix

DeFiMatrix is revolutionizing decentralized finance through AI-powered automation and intent-driven execution. Our platform combines cutting-edge web technologies with blockchain infrastructure to deliver a seamless DeFi experience.

## Role Overview

As a Full Stack Web3 Developer, you'll be responsible for building and maintaining the front-end and back-end components of our platform, with a focus on blockchain integration and Web3 functionality. You'll work on creating intuitive interfaces that make complex DeFi operations accessible to users.

## Key Responsibilities

- Develop and maintain our Next.js-based web application
- Implement Web3 functionality and blockchain integrations
- Build responsive and interactive user interfaces
- Create efficient APIs and backend services
- Integrate with smart contracts and blockchain networks
- Implement wallet connections and transaction handling
- Optimize application performance and user experience
- Write clean, maintainable, and well-tested code
- Collaborate with design and product teams

## Required Skills & Experience

- 3+ years of full-stack development experience
- Strong proficiency in React/Next.js and TypeScript
- Experience with Web3 libraries (ethers.js, web3.js)
- Knowledge of blockchain development and smart contracts
- Proficiency in backend development (Node.js, APIs)
- Understanding of state management (Redux, Context)
- Experience with responsive design and CSS frameworks
- Strong testing and debugging skills

## Nice to Have

- Experience with DeFi protocol integrations
- Knowledge of cross-chain development
- Familiarity with AI/ML integration
- Experience with GraphQL
- Understanding of tokenomics and DeFi mechanics
- Contributions to open-source projects
- Experience with WebAssembly or Rust

## What We Offer

- Competitive salary and equity package
- Opportunity to shape the future of DeFi UX
- Remote-first work environment with flexible hours
- Comprehensive health and wellness benefits
- Professional development and conference budget
- Access to cutting-edge Web3 technologies
- Collaborative and innovative team culture
