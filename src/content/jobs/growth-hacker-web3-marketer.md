# About DeFiMatrix

DeFiMatrix is revolutionizing decentralized finance through AI-powered automation and intent-driven execution. We're seeking a creative and data-driven Growth Hacker & Web3 Marketer to expand our user base and establish DeFiMatrix as a leader in the DeFi space.

## Role Overview

As Growth Hacker & Web3 Marketer, you'll be responsible for designing and implementing innovative growth strategies across Web3 channels. You'll combine traditional growth marketing techniques with crypto-native approaches to drive user acquisition and engagement.

## Key Responsibilities

- Design and execute growth marketing strategies for the DeFi ecosystem
- Create and optimize user acquisition funnels across Web3 platforms
- Develop and manage community-driven marketing campaigns
- Implement data-driven A/B testing and optimization strategies
- Coordinate with influencers and thought leaders in the crypto space
- Manage social media presence across crypto-native platforms
- Create engaging content for Web3 audiences
- Track and analyze key growth metrics and KPIs
- Collaborate with product teams on user retention strategies
- Develop and execute token-gated marketing campaigns

## Required Skills & Experience

- 3+ years of growth marketing experience in Web3/crypto
- Strong understanding of DeFi ecosystem and user behavior
- Experience with Web3 marketing tools and platforms
- Proven track record of driving user acquisition
- Data-driven approach to marketing optimization
- Experience with community-led growth strategies
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Excellent communication and content creation abilities

## Nice to Have

- Experience with token-incentivized growth programs
- Knowledge of tokenomics and incentive design
- Background in traditional growth marketing
- Understanding of blockchain technology
- Experience with DAO marketing
- Familiarity with AI/ML marketing tools
- Multi-language capabilities
