# About DeFiMatrix

DeFiMatrix is revolutionizing decentralized finance through AI-powered automation and intent-driven execution. We're building a vibrant ecosystem that brings together users, developers, and DAOs to create the future of decentralized finance.

## Role Overview

As Head of Community & DAO Relations, you'll lead our community engagement strategy and build strong relationships with DAOs and protocol partners. You'll be responsible for growing and nurturing our community while establishing DeFiMatrix as a key player in the DeFi ecosystem.

## Key Responsibilities

- Develop and execute community growth and engagement strategies
- Build and maintain relationships with key DAOs and protocols
- Create and manage community programs and initiatives
- Oversee community channels across Discord, Telegram, and other platforms
- Design and implement governance participation strategies
- Coordinate cross-DAO collaborations and partnerships
- Organize community events and educational programs
- Manage community moderator team
- Create community content and educational materials
- Drive protocol integration discussions with DAO partners

## Required Skills & Experience

- 5+ years of community management experience in Web3
- Strong understanding of DAO governance and operations
- Experience building and scaling online communities
- Knowledge of DeFi protocols and ecosystem
- Excellent communication and relationship-building skills
- Experience with community tools and platforms
- Strong leadership and team management abilities
- Understanding of tokenomics and incentive mechanisms

## Nice to Have

- Experience with DAO governance frameworks
- Multi-language capabilities
- Background in DeFi protocol operations
- Experience with token-gated communities
- Knowledge of community analytics tools
- Understanding of smart contract systems
- Experience with community-driven product development

## What We Offer

- Competitive salary and equity package
- Leadership role in shaping DeFi communities
- Remote-first work environment with flexible hours
- Comprehensive health and wellness benefits
- Professional development and conference budget
- Access to cutting-edge community tools
- Collaborative and innovative team culture
