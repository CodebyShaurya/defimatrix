# About DeFiMatrix

DeFiMatrix is revolutionizing decentralized finance through AI-powered automation and intent-driven execution. We're building a comprehensive ecosystem of protocols, partners, and integrations to provide users with the best possible DeFi experience.

## Role Overview

As Partnerships & Ecosystem Manager, you'll be responsible for building and maintaining strategic relationships across the DeFi ecosystem. You'll work on expanding DeFiMatrix's protocol integrations, establishing key partnerships, and growing our ecosystem of collaborators.

## Key Responsibilities

- Identify and pursue strategic partnership opportunities
- Manage relationships with DeFi protocols and projects
- Drive protocol integration discussions and negotiations
- Develop and execute ecosystem growth strategies
- Coordinate with technical teams on integration requirements
- Create and maintain partnership documentation
- Represent DeFiMatrix at industry events and conferences
- Build relationships with key stakeholders in the DeFi space
- Monitor ecosystem trends and opportunities
- Drive collaborative initiatives and joint ventures

## Required Skills & Experience

- 5+ years of business development or partnership management
- Strong understanding of the DeFi ecosystem
- Experience with protocol partnerships and integrations
- Excellent relationship-building abilities
- Strong negotiation and communication skills
- Strategic thinking and problem-solving abilities
- Experience with partnership lifecycle management
- Track record of successful partnerships

## Nice to Have

- Technical background in blockchain/DeFi
- Experience with DAO partnerships
- Knowledge of tokenomics and incentive design
- Background in financial services
- Multi-language capabilities
- Experience with developer relations
- Understanding of AI/ML technologies

## What We Offer

- Competitive salary and equity package
- Opportunity to shape DeFi ecosystem development
- Remote-first work environment with flexible hours
- Comprehensive health and wellness benefits
- Professional development and conference budget
- Travel opportunities for industry events
- Collaborative and innovative team culture
