# About DeFiMatrix

DeFiMatrix is revolutionizing decentralized finance through AI-powered automation and intent-driven execution. We're building a world-class team of innovators and need a People & Culture Lead who can help us create and maintain an exceptional workplace culture.

## Role Overview

As People & Culture Lead, you'll be responsible for developing and implementing strategies that attract, retain, and develop top talent while fostering an inclusive and innovative culture. You'll work on building a strong organizational foundation that supports our rapid growth and remote-first environment.

## Key Responsibilities

- Design and implement comprehensive HR strategies
- Lead recruitment and talent acquisition efforts
- Develop and manage employee engagement programs
- Create and maintain company culture initiatives
- Implement performance management systems
- Design competitive compensation and benefits packages
- Manage remote-first workplace policies
- Develop professional growth and learning programs
- Ensure compliance with employment regulations
- Foster diversity, equity, and inclusion initiatives
- Build and maintain HR processes and documentation
- Lead onboarding and team integration efforts

## Required Skills & Experience

- 5+ years of HR/People Operations experience
- Strong experience in talent acquisition and retention
- Knowledge of HR best practices and regulations
- Experience with remote-first organizations
- Strong leadership and communication abilities
- Experience with HR tools and platforms
- Track record of building inclusive cultures
- Understanding of startup environments

## Nice to Have

- Experience in Web3/crypto companies
- Knowledge of DAO structures and governance
- Background in tech industry HR
- Multi-language capabilities
- Experience with rapid scaling
- Certifications in HR management
- Experience with global workforce management

## What We Offer

- Competitive salary and equity package
- Opportunity to shape company culture
- Remote-first work environment with flexible hours
- Comprehensive health and wellness benefits
- Professional development and certification budget
- Access to industry-leading HR tools
- Collaborative and innovative team culture
