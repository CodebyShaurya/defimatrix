# User Research Lead (DeFi UX)

## Role Overview

We're seeking a User Research Lead to drive user-centered design and research initiatives at DeFiMatrix.io. This role is crucial in shaping our platform's user experience and ensuring our AI-powered DeFi solutions are intuitive and effective for users across all skill levels.

## Key Responsibilities

- Lead user research initiatives to understand DeFi user behaviors, needs, and pain points
- Design and conduct qualitative and quantitative research studies
- Improve natural language UX and AI model effectiveness through data-driven insights
- Collaborate with product, design, and engineering teams to implement research findings
- Create and maintain user personas, journey maps, and behavioral analytics
- Present research findings and recommendations to stakeholders

## Tools & Technologies

- Maze for user testing and prototype validation
- Dovetail for research organization and analysis
- Figma for collaborative design and prototyping
- Analytics platforms for quantitative data analysis
- User research and survey tools
- Session recording and heatmap tools

## Requirements

### Must-Have

- 3+ years of UX research experience in tech or fintech
- Strong background in qualitative and quantitative research methodologies
- Experience with user testing tools and analytics platforms
- Excellent communication and presentation skills
- Proven track record of driving product decisions through research
- Understanding of blockchain and DeFi concepts

### Nice-to-Have

- Experience in DeFi or crypto projects
- Background in AI/ML user experience research
- Knowledge of behavioral economics
- Experience with remote research methodologies
- International research experience
- Academic background in HCI, Psychology, or related fields

## Benefits

- Competitive salary and equity package
- Remote-first work environment
- Flexible working hours
- Health and wellness benefits
- Professional development budget
- Regular team retreats
- Home office setup allowance
- Crypto payments option

## Application Process

1. Initial application review
2. Portfolio presentation
3. Research methodology discussion
4. Team fit conversations
5. Final interview with leadership

To apply, please submit your portfolio, resume, and a brief description of your most impactful research project.
